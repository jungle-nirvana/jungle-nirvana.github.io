About Us

Junglenirvana is a Non-Profit Organization converting barren land into native, preservation free forest. Started in May 2020, Junglenirvana is a initiative of Shraddha Humanitarian society.

Our Purpose

To Convert barren land into forest boosting safe heaven for wildlife and grow Ecosystem which will create livelihood and path of nirvana for the community. Shraddha Humanitarian trust is governed by a Board of Trustees under its President, Dr Ajay Shesh.
