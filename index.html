About Us

Jungle Nirvana is a Non-Profit Organization converting barren land into native, preservation free forest. Started in May 2020, Jungle Nirvana is an initiative of Shraddha Humanitarian society.

Our Purpose

To Convert barren land into forest, boosting safe heaven for wildlife and growing ecosystem which will create livelihood for the community.
