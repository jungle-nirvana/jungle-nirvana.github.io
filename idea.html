Idea of Jungle Nirvana

Around 42 million hectares is classified as barren land in India and it is more than total area of Japan. At micro level, pockets of land can be converted to forest and associated ecosystem can be developed by using advance water management systems. Ecosystems will ensure self funding through forest produce and tourism. This will create safe heaven for wildlife, green land and livelihood for people.

Social Issues

  • Rivers

    Today more than forty percent of Indian rivers are depleted and fifty percent of Indian population is water distressed.

  • Land Degradation

    Due to land degradation currently more than 3 billion people are affected globally.

  • Economic Security

    To address the issue of economic security, water security, food security and climate change land degradation issue need to be addressed.

Solutions

  • Develop energetic Team

  • Identify barren land

  • Ensure resources are appropriately managed

  • Engage local government bodies and Society

  • Develop forest and water bodies on this land in the scientific way

  • Introduce medicinal plants and forest produce

  • Introduce eco tourism and develop ecosystem & deploy maintenance team

  • Provide system and framework for forest management

Benefits

  • Livelihood: This will generate employment to uneducated / semiskilled people.

  • Stop migration of people to cities. Make small villages self reliant.

  • Water Table: Improvement of water table in the region.

  • Model ecosystem which can be replicated across the India and globe.

  • Fulfillment of the commitment to Bonn challenge
