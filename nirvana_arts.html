Nirvana Arts

Nirvana is a place of perfect peace and happiness, like heaven. ... The origin of the word nirvana relates to religious enlightenment; it comes from the Sanskrit meaning "extinction, disappearance" of the individual to the universal. Achieving nirvana is to make earthly feelings like suffering and desire disappear.
