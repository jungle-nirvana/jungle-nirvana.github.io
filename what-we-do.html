Approach

We are team of qualified professionals from the different walks of the life came together, and a Mission “Jungle NirvanaTM” is envisaged to restore degraded land through sustainable business model by involving local people.

What We Do

The Process:

  • On long term basis lease the barren land from government.

  • Develop forest and water bodies on this land in the scientific way

  • Introduce Agroforestry and cultivate land through Permaculture

  • Involve society by developing associated ecosystem

Outcome

  • 1. Livelihood: This will generate employment to uneducated / semiskilled people. Stop migration of people to cities. Make small villages self reliant.

  • 2. Reclamation of forest lands : Safe heaven for wildlife habitat

  • 3. Water Table: Improvement of water table in the region.

  • 4. Model ecosystem which can be replicated across the India
